Phase 1 Stakeholders & Research Plan

- Primary users: casual users, professionals needing skin-tone references, accessibility-focused users
- Research goals: understand pain points with current sampling/results, validate clarity and trust priorities
- Methods: 1:1 interviews, quick usability tests, expert review of result presentation
- Interview guide (sample):
  - What are you trying to achieve with this tool?
  - How do you interpret the results today? What would improve it?
  - How important is privacy and on-device processing?
  - Which visuals help you understand skin tones best?
- Success criteria: clear interpretation, perceived accuracy, comfort with privacy controls, accessible interactions
- Participants: 6–12 across demographics relevant to skin-tone variation
